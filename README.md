# Nike Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

The Nike Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, product trends, and regional sales distribution. The dashboard provides key business insights through KPIs, trend analysis, geographical visualization, and product-level performance metrics.

## Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Analyze operating profit and profit margins.
- Identify top-performing products and retailers.
- Track monthly sales trends.
- Compare sales performance across regions and states.
- Support data-driven business decision making.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following metrics:

- **Total Sales:** $900M
- **Operating Profit:** $332M
- **Units Sold:** 2M
- **Average Price per Unit:** $45
- **Operating Margin:** 42%

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales Trend Analysis
- Monthly sales performance visualization.
- Identifies seasonal peaks and low-performing periods.
- Helps track revenue growth over time.

### 2. Geographic Sales Analysis
- Interactive map showing sales distribution across U.S. states.
- Enables regional performance comparison.
- Highlights high-revenue markets.

### 3. Regional Performance Analysis
- Sales contribution by region:
  - West
  - Northeast
  - Southeast
  - South
  - Midwest

### 4. Product Performance Analysis
- Identifies best-selling Nike product categories.
- Compares sales across different product segments.

### 5. Retailer Performance Analysis
- Evaluates revenue generated by major retailers.
- Highlights top-performing sales channels.

### 6. Interactive Filters
Users can filter data by:
- Region
- Invoice Date Range

## Business Insights Generated

- The West region generated the highest sales contribution.
- Monthly sales peaked during July and August.
- Men's Street Footwear emerged as the highest-selling product category.
- West Gear and Foot Locker were the leading retail partners.
- Strong operating margin indicates healthy profitability.
